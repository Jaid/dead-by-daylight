After you earn 3 Protection Hit scoring events, the perk activates.
Once activated, the next occasion that would put you into the dying state from the injured state is ignored.
The next time you heal back to full health, your aura will be revealed to the killer when you are further than {tiers:12/14/16} meters from the Killer.
Increases your chances of being the Killer's obsession.
The Killer can only be Obsessed with one survivor at a time.
