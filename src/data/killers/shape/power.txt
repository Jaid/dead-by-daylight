The darkness inside feeds his determination to take the lite of his prey. Activating Evil Within allows {shape} to see his prey clearly and build up more evil power by stalking them. Evil Within's 3 Tiers of power come with different benefits:

EVIL WITHIN I: Tremendously Decreased Terror Radius. Grants immunity to detection perks. Slightly decreased ovement speed. Slightly decreased lunge.
EVIL WITHIN II: Moderately Decreased Terror Radius. Slightly Increased movement speed. Slightly increased lunge.
EVIL WITHIN III: Normal Terror Radius. Hit Survivors suffer from the Exposed status effect. Evil Within III lasts for 60 seconds after which its power is considerably drained.
