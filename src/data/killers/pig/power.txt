Forever devoted to her masters cause, she punishes the ungrateful and the guilty with slyness and murderous puzzles. {pig} can move stealthily, dash in ambush attacks and put deadly Reverse Bear Traps on Survivors' heads

Use the Active Ability to assume a crouching position. While crouching:

• {pig} has no Terror Radius.
• Activate the attack interaction to dash and perform an ambush attack.

Start the trial with 4 Reverse Bear Traps:

• Reverse Bear Traps are put on dying Survivors. They start inactive.
• Reverse Bear Traps become active when any generator in the trial is completed.
• Active Reverse Bear Traps initiate a countdown timer.
• Active Reverse Bear Traps trigger once their timers expire, automatically sacrificing The Survivor.
• Active Reverse Bear Traps trigger automatically once
• Survivors can free themselves from Reverse Bear Traps by searching Jigsaw Boxes located in the environment to find the correct key.
