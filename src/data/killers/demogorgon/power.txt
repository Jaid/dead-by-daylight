• Of The Abyss: Press and hold the Power button to fully charge and activate Of The Abyss. All Survivors within a close proximity to a placed portal will be indicated by Killer Instinct.
• Shred: Press the Attack button while charging or holding Of The Abyss to dive forward and unleash a vicious slash.
• Portals: Press the Active Ability button to open a portal on the ground in front of you. While standing on a placed portal with a different portal highlighted, press the Active Ability button again to travel through the Upside Down to arrive at the targeted portal.
